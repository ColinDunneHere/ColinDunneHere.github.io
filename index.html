Mobilize

Digital Media, Photography & Photoshop, 2022

By March 2022, 4.3 million children in Ukraine had been displaced- more than half of the 7.5 million total child population. This piece is not to just communicate these numbers of the war, but to grip at the fact that this is a reality that these children live.

Icons of San Francisco

Digital Media, Illustrator, 2022

Emperor Norton, The Brown Twins, and Mr Smiley. These are a few of the local icons from the city of San Francisco. Though not on the scale of A-list celebrities that pass through SF from time to time, their significance and impact lie in their innate ability to make people smile.

Brand

Digital Media, Illustrator, 2022

A design series reflecting the disappointment of seeing USF’s relation with its part time teachers in the face of its expenditures on decorative infrastructure.

Accordion Book

Product Design Accordion Booklet, InDesign, 2022

A designed and produced booklet featuring the work of director James Cameron. Featuring the behind the scenes storyboards to his movies that have drawn him as an inspiration to me in curating projects, this production covers his background, work, and achievements from the beginning to his career to the published date.

David

Digital Media Design & Tattoo, Photoshop, 2022

I apprenticed in a tattoo shop for over two years, starting while I was still a high schooler. My first time tattooing skin was with a rose on my own leg. Placed on the opposite leg, this tattoo design of "David" was made to look up at the rose. At the time the rose was like Goliath to me, and now I wear it.

Wilhelm Klingspor Gotisch

Digital Media, InDesign, Research Poster Series (1/3), 30x40 in, 2022

This piece explores the history of the font Wilhelm Klingspor Gotisch and its creator Rudolf Koch. Its design derives inspiration from the media of its time under a contemporary eye.

Modern No. 20

Digital Media, InDesign, Research Poster Series (2/3), 30x40 in, 2022

This print project heavily sources imagery from the original in-house design magazine the font Modern No. 20 was published in and reflects on its history and creator Stephenson Blake. From color to composition, this piece derives inspiration from the past while applying new design concepts and methods.

Helvetica

Digital Media, InDesign, Research Poster Series (3/3), 30x40 in, 2022

This piece dives into the font created by Max Miedinger and Edüard Hoffmann, Helvetica, and how it has developed over time to impact design and harbor social and political implications lasting to this day.

After his fall

Digital Media, InDesign, 2022

With a challenging focus on typographical composition, this project repurposes text from "Divine Justice and The Human Order: An Islamic Perspective" by Azizah Y. al-Hibri to guide the design's inspiration and direction.

SIF Logo Design

Digital Media, 2025

A contracted start to finish logo design with the company Source International Fueling. Pitched ideas and iterations to the CEO and other team members, incorporating feedback to form hundreds of variations and ultimately create a final forefront basis of their corporate identity and imagery.

How To Make Scrambled Eggs

Video, 2023

An entertaining step-by-step "how to" tutorial meant to ease the intimidating process. Directed, filmed, and edited by me- the project features mixed media across narration, typography, and 3D animation.
